Invited Speakers

Valeria Ardizzone EGI Foundation, Amsterdam, The Netherlands

Valeria Ardizzone is IAM Solution Manager at the EGI Foundation, where she leads the Identity and Access Management area and oversees the operation of AAI services for the EGI Federation. With over 20 years of experience in European e-Infrastructures, she has served as a project manager, raining manager, cloud and grid computing specialist and researcher. A Computer Science graduate from the University of Catania (2003), Valeria combines deep technical expertise with a vision for trusted, user-centric solutions. Her recent passions focus on Data Spaces and on Governance and Trust policies, exploring their potential to transform collaboration, sovereignty, and innovation in research. She is dedicated to empowering communities by bridging trust, technology, and cross-disciplinary cooperation across Europe.

Jean-Karim Heriche Data Science Centre, European Molecular Biology Laboratory, Heidelberg, Germany

Jean-Karim Hériché is a scientist with over 20 years of experience in bioinformatics, computational biology and data science. He received his PhD in biology from Université Joseph Fourier in Grenoble and subsequently worked at the University of California, San Francisco and the Sanger Institute in Hinxton, before joining the European Molecular Biology Laboratory in Heidelberg where he is currently a staff scientist in the EMBL Data Science Centre. His main interests are extracting biological insights from dynamic image- derived data and developing a cloud-based ecosystem for bioimage informatics.
